## 4. Trial Strategy Analysis

### 7-Day Free Trial vs. No Trial

#### **Data-Driven Decision:**

**7-Day Free Trial (RECOMMENDED)**
- **Conversion Rate:** 45% (for 5-9 day trials)
- **User Psychology:** Risk-free, allows full feature exploration
- **LTV Impact:** Moderate (users commit after experiencing value)
- **Best For:** Feature-rich apps requiring behavior change

**No Trial (Direct Purchase)**
- **Conversion Rate:** Lower volume, but 2x LTV per customer
- **LTV Impact:** $60 vs $35-40 (70% increase)
- **User Psychology:** Self-selects highly motivated users
- **Best For:** Established brands with strong value proposition

## 10. Key Takeaways & Action Items

### Critical Success Factors

**1. Nail the Free Experience**
- Free tier must provide genuine value (not a frustrating trial)
- Core loop works without payment (log workout, see progress, set goals)
- Build trust and habit formation in first 7-14 days

**2. Strategic Feature Gating**
- Lock AI/intelligence behind Premium (not core tracking)
- Use soft paywalls (show what's possible, create desire)
- Don't gate social features (drives virality and retention)

**3. Optimize for Annual Subscriptions**
- 67% of H&F users choose annual (follow the data)
- Annual retains 36% vs 6.7% for monthly (5x better)
- Emphasize annual savings ($80 saved vs monthly)

**4. Trial Strategy**
- 7-day free trial converts at 45% (5-9 day sweet spot)
- No credit card required (reduces friction)
- Strong onboarding + email sequences = conversion

**5. Paywall Placement**
- Show early (during/after onboarding = 50% of trial starts)
- Use contextual triggers (before locked features)
- Don't be afraid to show value upfront (FitnessAI 2x revenue)

**6. Retention is Revenue**
- 97% churn by Day 30 (industry problem)
- Focus on Days 1-7 (habit formation window)
- Predictive churn detection + retention offers (discounts, pauses)

**7. Upsell to Premium+**
- Target power users (5+ workouts/week, 3+ months tenure)
- Trigger at key moments (plateau, tryout season)
- 5-10% conversion rate = high LTV segment

**8. Unit Economics**
- Target LTV:CAC >30:1 (aim for 40-50:1)
- Blended CAC: $3-5 per install
- Payback period: <30 days (ideally <15 days)
